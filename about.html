Welcome to our residence

The bedrooms at The Taj Garden Hotel are thoughtfully designed to provide guests with comfort, cleanliness, and relaxation. Each room features cozy bedding, elegant decor, and modern amenities such as air-conditioning, flat-screen TVs, Wi-Fi, and spacious wardrobes. Soft lighting and large windows allow natural light to brighten the space, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you choose a standard room or a deluxe suite, you’ll find everything you need for a peaceful and restful stay.

Special attention is given to cleanliness and maintenance to ensure every guest enjoys a fresh and hygienic environment. Some rooms also offer beautiful views of the surrounding gardens, enhancing the overall experience. With round-the-clock room service and attentive housekeeping, the hotel ensures that comfort and satisfaction are always a priority. The bedrooms reflect the hotel's commitment to providing a homely, calm, and pleasant space for travelers of all kinds.

Rooms

The rooms are designed to offer a perfect blend of comfort and calm. With soft beds, clean surroundings, and warm lighting, each space invites guests to relax and feel at home. Whether for rest or work, the rooms provide a peaceful retreat.

Garden

The garden is a quiet escape filled with greenery and fresh air. Neatly maintained paths, flowering plants, and seating areas make it ideal for morning walks or evening tea. It adds a natural charm to the hotel’s peaceful atmosphere.

Hall

The hall is spacious and well-lit, suitable for events, meetings, or casual gatherings. With elegant interiors and flexible arrangements, it can host both formal and informal occasions comfortably. It reflects the hotel’s welcoming spirit and functionality.
